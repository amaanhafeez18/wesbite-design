Located in South-Asia with a welcoming yet diverse climate

______________________

The south has a coastal climate with plentiful beaches dotting the southern borders. The west mainly consists of desert land and sandy hills.

The areas most heavily visited are the northern/northwestern provinces; these provinces, being high elevation, are home to much colder and pleasant temperatures, beautiful valleys and mountain ranges.

 

 

   

The People

______________________

South Asian hospitality, unrivaled. 

Hospitality is a strong aspect of Pakistani culture. It is not uncommon to be invited to a Pakistani's home without much prior acquaintance.

 

The spirit of Pakistani culture revolves around the practice of hospitality.
